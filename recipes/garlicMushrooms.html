Garlic Mushrooms

Description

Superquick, incredibly delicious recipe that anyone can make!

This serves 4 people, so its handy to have if you have people coming over with little time to prep.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 2 lbs sliced mushrooms
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 cup red wine

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add mushrooms and garlic; cook and stir until liquid has evaporated and mushrooms are light golden brown.
  3. Stir in the basil.
  4. Reduce heat to low.
  5. Pour wine into skillet.
  6. Simmer until wine has evaporated.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
