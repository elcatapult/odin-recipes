Roasted Broccoli

Description

Fairly simple recipe that should be easy to make by all!

Its quick to make and nutritious, as well as delicious!

Ingredients

  • Broccoli Florets
  • Broccoli Stems
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the broccoli florets from the stalk, and cut the stem into slices.
  2. Throw the stem pieces and florets into a pan with oil and toss them.
  3. Transfer to a baking sheet.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then roast in the oven as desired.
  5. Enjoy!
