Irish Soda Bread

Description

I used to thing this was made from actual soda when I was younger!

But its not! Its made from baking soda!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Margarine
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners (Baking Soda and Baking Powder)
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Egg
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, margarine, sugar, your leaveners, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Stir in the buttermilk and egg.
  3. Turn the dough and knead.
  4. Form into a round loaf.
  5. Brush with butter and buttermilk.
  6. Insert toothpick into center.
  7. Bake until toothpick comes out clean.
